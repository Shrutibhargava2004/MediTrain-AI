Diabetes Predictor

Diabetes is a disease that occurs when your blood glucose is too high. It can cause fatigue, blurry vision, and increased thirst.

Symptoms:
  • Frequent urination
  • Increased thirst
  • Fatigue

Heart Disease Predictor

Heart disease affects the heart's ability to pump blood effectively. It can lead to heart attacks, chest pain, and shortness of breath.

Symptoms:
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue

Alzheimer's Disease Predictor

Alzheimer's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that causes memory loss, confusion, and cognitive decline.

Symptoms:
  • Memory loss
  • Difficulty completing familiar tasks
  • Misplacing items and inability to retrace steps

Stroke Predictor

A stroke occurs when blood flow to part of the brain is blocked, which can lead to brain damage. It may cause sudden numbness, confusion, or difficulty walking.

Symptoms:
  • Sudden numbness
  • Difficulty speaking
  • Blurred vision

Asthma Predictor

Asthma is a chronic condition that affects the airways, causing difficulty in breathing. It can result in wheezing, coughing, and chest tightness.

Symptoms:
  • Shortness of breath
  • Coughing
  • Wheezing

Cancer Predictor

Cancer involves the uncontrolled growth of abnormal cells. It may lead to fatigue, unexplained weight loss, and persistent pain.

Symptoms:
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Persistent cough
  • Fatigue
