Diabetes Predictor

Diabetes is a disease that occurs when your blood glucose is too high. It can cause fatigue, blurry vision, and increased thirst.

Symptoms:
  • Frequent urination
  • Increased thirst
  • Fatigue

Heart Disease Predictor

Heart disease affects the heart's ability to pump blood effectively. It can lead to heart attacks, chest pain, and shortness of breath.

Symptoms:
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue

Alzheimer's Disease Predictor

Alzheimer's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that causes memory loss, confusion, and cognitive decline.

Symptoms:
  • Memory loss
  • Difficulty completing familiar tasks
  • Misplacing items and inability to retrace steps
