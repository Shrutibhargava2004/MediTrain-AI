What is MediTrain AI?

MediTrain AI is an innovative healthcare assistant designed to simulate real-life patient interactions and provide AI-powered diagnostic support. It enables users, especially medical professionals and students, to practice their diagnostic skills with an intelligent chatbot acting as a simulated patient.

Key Features

  • Simulated Patient Conversations
  • AI-driven Diagnostic Assistance
  • Medical Condition Analysis
  • Session-based Conversation History
  • Interactive Chat for Real-time Learning

How Does It Work?

  • The AI presents itself as a patient with symptoms.
  • Users (acting as doctors) ask follow-up questions.
  • The AI responds based on medical data and logic.
  • The user suggests a diagnosis and possible treatment.
  • The AI acknowledges and concludes the session.
